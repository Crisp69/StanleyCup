If you select your team in the list, your team and players from your team will be highlighted in the players and team stats pages, so you will not have to search them too hard.



This will be done by saving a cookie in your PC and only information which will be stored will be your team name. you can any time change your selection in the list. If you use several PC to come to Stanley Cup web, you have to make the selection at each of your PCs as this information is directly saved on PC harddisk.
