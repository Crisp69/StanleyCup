Main page

• Use teams logos on the top to navigate to HA detail teams info page

• Click Stanley Cup Ice Girls picture on the top right to navigate to picture gallery

• 3 new magazine articles in the right column of the main page – fresh articles with red title

• Click more at the bottom of Stars of the weekend for full list of Stars of the weekend selection



News

• Only first paragraph of the news is displayed on main page, for more details click news title or [read more] link below text.

• Navigate to older news through number of pages below list of news



Teams

• On summary page use links conference teams comparison for HA-tools conference comparison

• Use the selector on top right to navigate to another teams details

• Use the selector below HA-tools logo to see head-to-head match up history against selected team (go to head-to-head match up history page, select teams through selectors menu on the top)

• Teams history per season in displayed here

• Teams and teams players / managers trophies are displayed here – click trophy image for full list of trophy winners, click players name for full players Stanley Cup stats, click number of season for full list of trophies in that season



Schedule

• Read [challenge help] for challenge sending instructions

• Use selector view schedule by team to display games of desired teams only

• Use selector on top right to navigate to another seasons and or regular season or playoffs

• Click calendar icon at the end of each row for head-to-head match up history of teams in the row

• Click team logo to navigate to teams detail page

• Click team name to navigate to HA team info page

• Use home – away links to send challenges to team above (from your point of view)



Standings

• Use selector on top right to navigate to another season standings

• Click team logo to navigate to teams detail page

• Click team name to navigate to HA team info page

• Click calendar icon at the end of each row to navigate to regular season schedule of that team and season



Stats

• Use top navigation to make your selection

• selecting position other than all will display all players sorted by position

• selecting team will show all players stats per team and position (if position = all, all positions will be displayed)

• at the bottom of seasons list you can select TOP 500 historical stats (if all positions, number will be reduced as usual)

• Clicking name of position in the title of stats table (in all positions selection) will display all players per position

• Clicking players name will display players historical stats per season

• Clicking teams name will display teams players stats per position (if teams players stats displayed, clicking on the team will navigate to teams detail page)

• Other selection than All positions and All teams – stats are sort able by columns, click the title of the column to sort



Magazine

• Only first paragraph of the article is displayed on main page, for more details click article title or [read more] link below text.

• Use --> Write your own articles here! <-- to navigation to article add form

• Clicking on manager name in the heading of the article will open HA mail to manager

• Add comments to the article through the form below the article (see number of comments in the list of articles…)

• Clicking on teams name in the heading of the article will navigate to teams detail page

• Navigate to older articles through number of pages below list of articles



Awards

• Awards name and picture navigates to list of historical winners of the award

• Clicking on the teams name will navigate to teams detail page

• Clicking on the players name will navigate to players stats per season

• Clicking on the number of season in award detail page will navigate to full list of awards in the season

• Use top right navigation to select season (or trophy in detail awards page)



Hall of fame

• Click team name and manager name to navigate to HA team / manager detail page
